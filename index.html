Erica Stanley (@ericastanley) is a Senior Research Engineering Manager at Mozilla, entrepreneur, and tech diversity, equity and inclusion advocate. Working passionately towards gender and multicultural inclusion in tech, Erica co-founded the Atlanta network of Women Who Code, where she leads new developer workshops and organizes monthly tech talks, hack nights, and networking events for women in tech. To help improve strategies around diversity and inclusion, she also collaborates with companies within the Atlanta tech community and sits on the advisory board of 100 Girls of Code.
